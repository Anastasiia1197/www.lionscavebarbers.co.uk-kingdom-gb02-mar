Lions Cave Barbers Privacy Policy – Transparency and Protection of Your Data

Last updated: 22 January 2026

At Lions Cave Barbers, we place great importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our booking services and online platform in the United Kingdom 🇬🇧. We are fully transparent about our data processing practices and operate in accordance with stringent UK and European privacy regulations, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

What data we collect and how it improves your experience

To provide you with the best grooming experience and efficient appointment management on our platform, we collect specific personal data. This data helps us manage your bookings, improve our services, and provide you with a safe and personalised experience.

Personal data enabling identification

  • Name and contact information (for scheduling appointments and sending reminders)
  • Email address (if you subscribe to our grooming tips, newsletters, or loyalty program)
  • Appointment history to provide a more tailored service during your next visit

Technical and usage data

  • IP address and device information for security and online booking integrity
  • Browser type and version to ensure our booking system displays correctly on your device
  • Interaction data with our website to understand which services are most popular
  • Cookies and tracking data to remember your preferences

All our data collection activities comply with UK 🇬🇧 privacy regulations and aim to improve your experience with Lions Cave Barbers. We never collect more data than is strictly necessary for providing our premium grooming services.

The role of cookies in personalising your experience

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to optimise your experience on Lions Cave Barbers website. They play a key role in providing a secure and seamless booking environment for our clients in the United Kingdom.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the booking system to function and for site security.
  • Functional cookies: Store your preferences such as your preferred barber or location.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how clients use our site so we can improve our online presence.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the effectiveness of our promotions (always with your consent).

Managing cookies

You retain full control over cookie settings via your browser or our cookie banner. Please note: disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to book appointments online via the Lions Cave Barbers website. Our settings comply with the UK PECR and GDPR regulations.

How we responsibly use your data to improve our services

Your data is used only for legitimate business purposes that improve your experience at Lions Cave Barbers and guarantee service reliability.

Purpose Data Usage Legal Basis
Appointment management Scheduling, confirmations, and reminders for your grooming sessions. Contract performance
Website functionality Technical data enabling a stable and fast booking experience. Legitimate interest
Customer support Contact details to respond to your queries or reschedule appointments. Contract performance
Marketing & Loyalty Personalised offers and grooming updates for our regular clients. Consent
Security IP address analysis to prevent spam or fraudulent bookings. Legitimate interest

We process your data solely for the specific purposes for which it was collected. Our data processing complies with the principles of purpose limitation and data minimisation required by law in the UK 🇬🇧.

Rigorous data sharing protocols with trusted partners

Lions Cave Barbers shares your data only with carefully selected partners when necessary to provide our services (e.g., booking software providers or payment processors). All partners must meet our rigorous privacy standards.

Categories of data recipients

  • Booking Software Providers: To manage our calendar and your appointments securely.
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics): To understand site usage and improve user experience.
  • Communication Tools: For sending SMS reminders or email confirmations regarding your visits.
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by UK law or for the protection of our rights.

International data transfers

If data is transferred outside the UK or EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses. Our data processing always remains compliant with UK data protection law.

Your rights to access, correct, or delete your data

Under the UK GDPR, you have extensive rights regarding your personal data. Lions Cave Barbers fully respects these rights and provides simple procedures to exercise them.

Your privacy rights

  • Right of access: See what information we hold about your appointments.
  • Right to rectification: Update your contact details or preferences.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your client profile ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction: Limit how we use your data in specific scenarios.
  • Right to data portability: Request a copy of your data in a digital format.
  • Right to object: Stop receiving marketing communications at any time.

How to exercise your rights

  1. Manage your profile directly through our booking partner's interface.
  2. Submit a request via our online contact form.
  3. Contact our Data Protection Officer directly using the email address below.
  4. Provide proof of identity so we can ensure your data is handled securely.
  5. We will respond to your request within the statutory period of 30 days.

For questions regarding your privacy rights, you can contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@lionscavebarbers.co.uk. All requests are handled in accordance with UK data protection law.

Keeping you informed – updates to our privacy policy

We regularly review our privacy policy to adapt to new legal requirements or improvements to our barbering services. We assure you that you will be informed of significant changes.

Notification of changes

  • The 'Last updated' date at the top of this policy will be changed accordingly.
  • Major changes will be communicated via our website or via email to registered clients.
  • The most current version will always be available on this page.

Contact Details

For questions regarding this privacy policy or our data processing, you can contact our privacy team at dpo@lionscavebarbers.co.uk or by post: Lions Cave Barbers, FAO Data Protection Officer, High Street 45, London, WC1A 1AA, United Kingdom 🇬🇧.
